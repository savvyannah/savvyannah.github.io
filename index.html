Bringing visions to life is a passion of mine, given that I have been a clairvoyant for my whole life.

I have actively embraced my gifts in the last two decades, ensuring that I allow myself to get to know my gifts and enhancing the best ways to use them in order to help others.

I have been a Reiki practitioner since 2006, and a master for more than four years. I have combined this with my intuitive knowledge of crystals and affirmations to bring about my Aqualight Vibrational Crystal Drops range, which I have created to help people remove blocks in their lives and continue on their journey with comfort and happiness.

One of my favourite tools of the trade are Oracle cards and Tarot cards, which allow me to connect with spirits using my "clairs".

Since 2011 I have widened my client base, and have started teaching children how to use affirmations and meditation in their lives. This has been particularly rewarding as I have received some amazing feedback and have seen many beautiful changes, with children becoming more confident within themselves after spending time in my workshops.

In 2012 I completed training with Doreen Virtue and Radleigh Valentine to become a certified Angel Card Reader, which has further heightened my abilities.

I am passionate about my gifts, and experience great satisfaction knowing the spiritual connections I can bring to my clients.

I feel so fortunate to bear my gifts, and I hope that my clients feel similarly rewarded during our sessions.
